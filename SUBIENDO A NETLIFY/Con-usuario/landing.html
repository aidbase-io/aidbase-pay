Features

Everything your organization needs,
nothing you don't

Unlike generic tools, AidBase is designed specifically for the daily reality of social aid organizations.

👥
Beneficiary database

One record per person, shared across all projects. Auto-fills forms when they enter their ID — no re-registration ever.

✍️
Digital signatures

Beneficiaries sign on any phone or tablet. Signatures are saved with a timestamp, hash and PDF export — legally traceable.

📊
Project statistics

See beneficiaries by family status, city, children and delivery date. Print reports or export to PDF in one click.

📋
Form builder

Create registration and delivery forms with drag-and-drop. Each form gets a shareable link and QR code instantly.

📱
QR codes for events

Print the QR code and place it at your event. Beneficiaries scan and fill the form on their own phone — no paper needed.

🔒
User roles & access

Admin, Operator, and Read-only roles. Give your team exactly the access they need — no more, no less.

🌐
Multilingual forms

Multilingual support with RTL built-in. Designed for the diverse communities you serve.

📥
Import & export

Import from Excel or CSV with duplicate detection. Export to CSV, JSON or PDF at any time. Your data, always accessible.

🏷️
Organization branding

Upload your logo and it appears on all public forms. Beneficiaries see your organization, not a generic tool.

How it works

From setup to first signature
in under an hour

1
Set up your workspace

Upload your logo, create your aid projects (Food Basket, Pesach, Emergency Kits...) and invite your team. Takes 10 minutes.

2
Share the registration form

Send the link via WhatsApp or print the QR code. Beneficiaries register themselves — data goes straight into your database.

3
Collect signatures at the event

At the event, beneficiaries enter their ID and sign on the spot. Auto-identification fills their data in seconds.

4
View reports & statistics

See who signed, who didn't, statistics by category, and export everything for your records or funders.
